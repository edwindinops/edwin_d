  • HISTORY


  • The history of coffee industry dates back to the 15th century when coffee was first discovered in the Middle East and was drunk by the people there. Since then, coffee has been a cornerstone of culture and trade for centuries.The Dutch were among the first Europeans to import coffee from the Middle East and established coffee houses in Europe.Later during the 1700s, coffee became a major part of the trans-atlantic slave trade, as coffee estates were built in latin american countries, fueling the demand for labor. The 19th century saw the growthof coffee empire, with companies like Nescafe and Maxwell house selling package coffeess throughout Europe and North America. Today, coffee is one of the most popular beverages in the world and a huge economic forces.
